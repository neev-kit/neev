# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We pledge that everyone participating in the Neev community and its communities will be treated with respect and dignity.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that creates a positive environment include:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Being respectful in code reviews and discussions
- Reporting issues through proper channels

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Offensive language or jokes about protected characteristics
- Personal attacks or insults
- Harassment or intimidation
- Unwelcome advances or attention
- Trolling or inflammatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Abuse of power or authority
- Discrimination based on identity

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

Project maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject:
- Comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions
- Communications that are not aligned with this Code of Conduct

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces where Neev members interact:
- Repository (GitHub issues, discussions, pull requests)
- GitHub Discussions
- Email communications
- Community events
- Social media when representing the Neev community

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team at:
- **GitHub Issues**: Report via [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/neev-kit/neev/issues)
- **Direct**: Contact project maintainers via private message
- **Email**: neev-maintainers@github.com (or through GitHub)

### Investigation

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. All project team members are obligated to respect the privacy and security of the reporter of any incident.

### Consequences

Depending on the severity and pattern of behavior, consequences may include:

1. **Warning** — Verbal or written warning with expectation of improved behavior
2. **Temporary Suspension** — Suspension from community for a defined period
3. **Permanent Ban** — Removal from the community and all associated spaces
